Last Updated: December 21, 2025

Introduction

DomeLearn ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and providing transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use when you use the DomeLearn mobile application ("the App") - an educational platform for learning any subject through scrollable, bite-sized content.

By using DomeLearn, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Identifier (Email Address):

  • We collect the email address you provide during registration
  • This email serves as your unique Account ID to save and synchronize your learning progress, preferences, and Bootcamp status across devices
  • Important: We do not verify email addresses. You are responsible for the security and accuracy of the identifier you choose to use

User-Generated Content:

  • Custom Lessons: JSON files containing educational content (lessons, definitions, analogies, code examples, diagrams) that you upload through the "Personal Lessons" feature
  • Personal Topics: Custom learning topics and categories you create
  • Lesson Templates: Content you generate using our lesson creation tools

Learning Preferences:

  • Selected courses (e.g., Python, JavaScript, Mathematics, History, etc.)
  • Chosen difficulty levels (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)
  • Bootcamp selections and progression status
  • Course unlock preferences

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Learning Progress and Performance Data:

  • Lesson interactions: Posts you like, mark as "understood," "learned," or "mastered"
  • Quiz performance: Scores, pass/fail status, question responses, time spent on quizzes
  • Course completion status and mastery levels
  • Learning velocity and progress trends over time
  • Topic mastery tracking (up to 50 topics per user)

Usage Data:

  • App interactions and feature usage (which screens you visit, how often)
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Navigation patterns within the app
  • Scroll behavior and content engagement metrics

Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version
  • App version and build number
  • Device language settings
  • Screen resolution (for optimal content display)

Performance and Diagnostic Data:

  • Crash reports and error logs
  • App performance metrics (loading times, responsiveness)
  • Technical debugging information (to improve app stability)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Service Provision

  • Account Management: Use your email as a unique identifier to maintain your account and sync data across devices
  • Personalized Learning Experience: Customize your content feed based on your selected courses, levels, and Bootcamp preferences
  • Progress Tracking: Save and display your learning progress, quiz scores, and mastery achievements
  • Bootcamp Mode: Track your sequential course progression and automatically unlock new courses when you master prerequisites

2.2 Content Management

  • Custom Content Integration: Process and display your uploaded JSON lessons in the main learning feed
  • Topic Management: Allow you to create, modify, and delete up to 50 personal learning topics
  • Content Recommendations: Suggest relevant lessons based on your progress and preferences

2.3 Performance Analytics

  • Personal Analytics: Generate detailed statistics about your learning performance
  • App Improvement: Analyze aggregated, anonymized usage data to improve app features and user experience

2.4 Communication

  • Service Updates: Send important notifications about your account or the service
  • Support Responses: Respond to your questions, feedback, and support requests
We do NOT use your data for advertising, marketing emails, or promotional purposes.

3. User Control and Data Management

DomeLearn gives you full, real-time control over your data:

3.1 Content Management Rights

Add, Modify, and Delete:

  • You can add new custom lessons at any time through the Personal Lessons feature
  • You can modify or update your uploaded content
  • You can bulk-delete learning topics (up to your 50-topic limit) directly within the app
  • All changes take effect immediately and are synchronized with our servers

Preference Management:

  • Change your selected courses at any time from the Preferences screen
  • Switch between individual course mode and Bootcamp mode
  • Adjust difficulty level selections
  • All preference changes immediately update your learning feed and reset the associated preference data in our database

3.2 Data Minimization Commitment

  • We collect only the data necessary to provide the DomeLearn service
  • Your learning preferences and progress data are used exclusively for your personal educational experience
We do NOT:
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Track you across other apps or websites
  • Sell, rent, or trade your personal information
  • Share your learning progress with others without your explicit consent

4. Data Retention and Deletion

4.1 Retention Policy

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account remains active to provide continuous service
  • Inactive Accounts: If you stop using the app but do not delete your account, we retain your data to allow you to return and resume your progress
  • Deleted Content: When you delete custom lessons or topics within the app, they are permanently removed from our servers within 30 days

4.2 Account and Data Deletion

You have the right to request complete deletion of your account and all associated data.

Method 1: In-App Request
Contact us via the support email provided in the "About" screen within the app.

Method 2: Web-Based Request
Visit our account deletion page: Account Deletion Request Form

Method 3: Direct Email
Send an email to: asadrabbani1319@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"

Required Information:
Include the email address (Account ID) associated with your DomeLearn account.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account:

  • Your account identifier (email) is permanently deleted
  • All learning progress, quiz results, and performance data are erased
  • All custom-uploaded lessons and personal topics are removed
  • Preference settings and Bootcamp progress are cleared
  • This action is irreversible and cannot be undone

Processing Timeline: Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it:

5.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Secure Storage: Your data is stored on encrypted, secure servers with restricted access
  • Authentication: We use token-based authentication to verify your identity and protect your account
  • Regular Security Audits: We periodically review our security practices to identify and address potential vulnerabilities

5.2 User Responsibility

Account Security: Since we do not verify email addresses, you are responsible for:

  • Choosing a secure account identifier
  • Keeping your login credentials confidential
  • Reporting any unauthorized access to your account immediately

Note: While we implement strong security measures, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
